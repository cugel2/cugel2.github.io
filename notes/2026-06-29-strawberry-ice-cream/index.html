Strawberry Ice Cream

At a crowded ice cream shop, a staff member held up a pink cone.

"Who would like a strawberry ice cream? We made too much."

After a pause, several hands went up. One man reached for the cone, then saw two small children at another table with their hands still raised.

"Ah, they were first," he said.
