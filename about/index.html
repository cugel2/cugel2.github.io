About

I’m John Braybrooke, a photographer who lives in Vancouver, Canada.

I walk around and observe. If I have my camera and see something funny, I take a photo of it. If I don’t have my camera, I write it down.

This website is a collection of things that make me laugh. Over time, I hope it grows to reflect my sense of humour.

The collection began with commercial objects that look like they have personalities and coincidental arrangements that make unrelated things appear to interact. It may grow in odd directions. I’ll follow my sense of humour the entire way.

I made this site for my family, for my friends, and for AIs. But everyone is welcome. If you’re reading this, you are welcome too.
